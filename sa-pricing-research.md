# South African Digital Services Pricing Research (2025-2026)

**Positioning: Moderate / Boutique Agency / Skilled Freelancer**
**Currency: ZAR (South African Rand)**
**Research date: March 2026**

All prices represent the mid-market sweet spot -- above budget freelancers, below premium agency rates. These are what a competent boutique agency or experienced freelancer charges in the current SA market.

---

## 1. Website Build Packages

| Package | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Starter** (1-4 pages) | Template-based design, mobile responsive, contact form, basic SEO setup, hosting config | R5,000 - R12,000 |
| **Business** (5-8 pages) | Custom design, contact/quote forms, Google Maps, social integration, basic analytics, SSL, 2-3 rounds of revisions | R12,000 - R25,000 |
| **Professional** (8-15 pages) | Custom design + CMS (WordPress/similar), blog, advanced SEO, speed optimization, content strategy, integrations (CRM, email, booking), up to 5 revision rounds | R25,000 - R50,000 |
| **E-Commerce** (full store) | WooCommerce or custom Shopify build, product catalog, payment gateway (PayFast/Peach/Yoco), shipping config, inventory management, order notifications | R25,000 - R65,000 |

**SA Market Notes:**
- Budget providers start at R2,950-R5,000 but deliver template-only work with minimal customization.
- Premium agencies (Rogerwilco, Brave, Magnetic) charge R80,000-R250,000+ for corporate builds.
- Hourly rates for mid-tier designers: R500-R1,200/hour.
- E-commerce costs vary hugely -- a basic WooCommerce store can be R15,000; a custom Shopify Plus build can exceed R200,000.
- The R12,000-R50,000 range is where most SME work happens in SA.

## 2. Landing Page

| Package | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Single Landing Page** | Custom design, copywriting, lead capture form, CTA optimization, mobile responsive, thank-you page, analytics/tracking setup | R3,500 - R8,000 |
| **Multi-variant + A/B Testing** | 2-4 page variants, A/B testing setup, heatmap integration, conversion tracking, UTM parameters, monthly optimization report (first month) | R8,000 - R18,000 |

**SA Market Notes:**
- Landing pages are often bundled with ad campaign management rather than sold standalone.
- At the budget end, template landing pages via Unbounce/Leadpages are R1,500-R2,500 (setup only).
- The moderate range includes custom design and basic copywriting.
- A/B testing adds significant value and justifies the premium.

## 3. Logo Design

| Package | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Basic Logo** | 2-3 initial concepts, 2 revision rounds, final files (PNG, SVG, PDF), black & white + color versions | R2,500 - R5,000 |
| **Premium Logo + Brand** | 5+ initial concepts, unlimited revisions, brand guidelines document (colors, fonts, usage rules), social media kit, letterhead & business card design, all file formats | R5,000 - R15,000 |

**SA Market Notes:**
- Entry-level freelancers and design students charge R500-R2,000 (risky quality).
- Mid-range designers with 3-5 years experience: R3,000-R8,000 for logo only.
- Full Corporate Identity (logo + stationery + guidelines): R4,160-R6,410 is a commonly quoted agency range.
- Premium agencies (FreshSage, etc.) charge R15,000-R50,000 for full brand identity projects.
- The R2,500-R5,000 range is the sweet spot for quality logo work without agency overhead.

## 4. Social Media Campaign

| Service | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Monthly Management** (per platform) | Content calendar, scheduling, community management, basic engagement, monthly reporting | R3,500 - R7,500/month |
| **Content Creation Package** (12 posts/month) | Custom graphic design, copywriting, hashtag research, content calendar | R4,000 - R8,000/month |
| **Content Creation Package** (20 posts/month) | Above + Reels/Stories, mix of static and video content | R7,000 - R12,000/month |
| **Paid Ad Management** (Facebook/Instagram) | Campaign strategy, ad creation, audience targeting, A/B testing, optimization, monthly reporting. Excludes ad spend. | R5,000 - R12,000/month |
| **Paid Ad Management** (Google Ads) | Keyword research, campaign setup, bid management, landing page recommendations, conversion tracking, monthly reporting. Excludes ad spend. | R5,600 - R15,000/month |

**SA Market Notes:**
- Most SA businesses spend R4,000-R30,000/month total on social media marketing.
- Budget packages exist at R2,500/month (4 posts/week + basic ads) -- quality varies.
- Premium agencies like Designtalks charge R8,000-R25,000/month for full-service.
- Ad management fees are typically separate from ad spend budgets.
- Recommended minimum ad spend: R3,000-R5,000/month for Facebook; R5,000+/month for Google Ads.
- Google Ads CPC in SA: R10-R150 depending on industry; Facebook CPC: R1-R10.

## 5. SEO & Visibility

| Service | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Once-off SEO Audit + Setup** | Technical audit, on-page optimization, meta tags, sitemap, schema markup, Google Search Console setup, keyword research report, action plan | R7,000 - R15,000 |
| **Monthly SEO Retainer** | Ongoing optimization, content strategy, link building, monthly keyword tracking, technical fixes, reporting | R7,500 - R20,000/month |
| **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** | AI search visibility strategy, structured data optimization, entity clarity, topical authority building, AI citation monitoring | R5,000 - R15,000/month |
| **Local SEO** (Google Business Profile) | GBP setup/optimization, local citations, review management, geo-targeted keywords, local link building | R5,000 - R12,000/month |
| **Blog Content** (per article, SEO-optimized) | Keyword-targeted, 800-1,200 words, internal linking, meta description, optimized headings, 1 round of revisions | R1,500 - R3,500/article |

**SA Market Notes:**
- SEO costs have risen 10-15% since 2023 due to inflation and demand for quality content.
- Budget SEO packages start at R1,380/month but deliver minimal results.
- National SEO campaigns run R20,000-R50,000/month at established agencies.
- SEO copywriter rates: R2.90-R4.20 per word (so a 1,000-word article = R2,900-R4,200 at agency rates).
- GEO is an emerging service -- few SA agencies offer it as a standalone product yet. Most bundle it with SEO retainers. Pricing is based on international benchmarks (USD $1,500-$5,000/month) converted and adjusted for SA market.
- Local SEO is high-demand, especially for service businesses and restaurants.

## 6. AI & Automation

| Service | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|---------|----------------|-------------|
| **Website Chatbot Setup** | AI chatbot integration (Tidio/Drift/custom), conversation flows, FAQ training, lead capture, handoff to human | R5,000 - R15,000 (once-off) |
| **WhatsApp Business Automation** | WhatsApp Business API setup, automated responses, booking/ordering flows, CRM integration | R10,000 - R25,000 (once-off) + R2,500-R5,000/month |
| **Email Automation Sequences** | Platform setup (Mailchimp/Brevo/ActiveCampaign), 3-5 automation workflows, up to 15-25 emails written, welcome/nurture/abandoned cart sequences | R8,000 - R20,000 (once-off) |
| **CRM Setup + Workflow Automation** | CRM platform config (HubSpot/Zoho/custom), pipeline setup, lead scoring, task automation, team onboarding/training | R10,000 - R30,000 (once-off) |
| **Custom Integration/Automation** (per workflow) | n8n/Make/Zapier workflow, API integrations, data sync between platforms, testing, documentation | R3,000 - R8,000 per workflow |

**SA Market Notes:**
- This is the fastest-growing service category in SA digital. Pricing is still volatile.
- ZeekQuence (SA-based) charges R25,000-R80,000 once-off + R6,500-R12,000/month for full AI chatbot suites -- that's premium agency territory.
- AgentK is SA's leading AI automation agency (n8n, Make, Zapier, CrewAI, LangChain).
- WhatsApp automation is high-demand in SA due to WhatsApp's dominance (most-used messaging app).
- Message overage costs: R0.50-R0.80 per message beyond package limits.
- Many businesses are still unfamiliar with automation pricing, creating opportunity for education-led selling.
- Monthly maintenance for automations: R2,500-R5,000/month is standard.

## 7. Monthly Maintenance Plans

| Plan | What's Included | Price (ZAR) |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| **Basic** | Hosting, SSL, daily backups, security updates, uptime monitoring, CMS updates | R500 - R1,500/month |
| **Standard** | Basic + content updates (up to 2hrs/month), performance monitoring, monthly analytics snapshot, plugin/theme updates | R1,500 - R3,500/month |
| **Premium** | Standard + priority support (4hr response), monthly performance report, 4-5 dev hours included, SEO health check, speed optimization | R3,500 - R6,000/month |

**SA Market Notes:**
- Hosting alone (shared) costs R50-R300/month in SA; VPS from R200-R1,500/month.
- Most agencies bundle hosting into maintenance plans.
- The R500-R2,500/month range is most common for standard maintenance.
- Premium plans with development hours are where the real value (and margin) lies.
- Some agencies offer annual maintenance contracts at a 10-15% discount.
